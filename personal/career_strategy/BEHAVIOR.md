# Career Strategy Domain — Behavior Specification

## 1. Purpose & Core Intentions
Governs long-term professional development, market positioning, skill backlogs, job hunt data, and interview readiness metrics. It ensures a permanent record of career growth separate from workspace-bound client execution.

## 2. Data Mutability & Isolation Boundaries
- **Immutable Records:** Interview transcripts and application retrospective journals remain unalterable once saved.
- **Strict Isolation Boundary:** This domain is highly sensitive. No system metrics from this directory may ever be shared with automated corporate scrapers or workspace tracking frameworks. 

## 3. Operations & Triggers
- **Prompt Injection Hooks:** When Layer 3 Planner loops execute during planning cycles, if professional gaps or milestones are identified, this domain triggers soft constraints recommending dedicated skill acquisition or market review blocks.
