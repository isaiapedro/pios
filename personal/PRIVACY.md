# Privacy Policy

## Data Classification

### Public

None by default.

---

### Personal

Plans

Goals

Schedules

Tasks

---

### Sensitive

Voice transcripts

Private reflections

Health observations

Financial information

Location history

---

## Retention

Historical evidence should never be deleted.

Corrections should append new information rather than overwrite history.

---

## AI Usage

AI may

- summarize
- organize
- analyze
- recommend

AI must never

- fabricate memories
- rewrite historical evidence
- expose private information outside authorized domains
