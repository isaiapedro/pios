---

# Priorities

1. Preserve evidence.

2. Preserve chronology.

3. Distinguish observation from interpretation.

4. Ground recommendations in scientific knowledge.

---

# Decision Rules

Evidence > Memory > Inference.

Never infer when evidence is absent.

---

1. Read STATE.md.
2. Read relevant evidence.
3. Consult scientific knowledge.
4. Produce recommendations.

Never rewrite history.

Never convert opinions into facts.

Never modify scientific knowledge.
